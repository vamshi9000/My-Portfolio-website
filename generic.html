PIZZA SALES REPORT USING POWER BI

Your Portfolio

The "PizzaSales" Power BI dashboard project involved creating an interactive and visually appealing report to analyze sales data for a pizza business. The project included integrating data from various sources, such as sales records and customer information, and transforming it using Power Query. Key metrics such as total sales, sales by region, top-selling items, and customer demographics were visualized through dynamic charts and graphs. Advanced DAX measures were used to calculate key performance indicators (KPIs) and trends. The dashboard provided actionable insights, helping the business track performance and make data-driven decisions to improve sales and customer engagement.
